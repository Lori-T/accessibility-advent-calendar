Day 1: Add Alt Text to Images

Alt text provides descriptions for images that help screen readers convey their purpose to users with visual impairments.

How to write good alt text

  • Describe the purpose of the image in context.
  • Be concise but descriptive.
  • Avoid redundant phrases like "image of" or "picture of."
